multiple specialists and assistants, and the men working on the site The novel is noted most for its careful description of the dig site and house, which showed that the author had spent much of her time in very similar situations The characters in this book in particular are also based on archaeologists whom Christie knew from her personal experiences on excavation sites ''[[Death on the
