battle-cry must be: "The Permanent Revolution" }[[File:1919-Trotsky Lenin Kamenev-Party-Congress pg|thumb|right|Trotsky, [[Vladimir Lenin]] and [[Lev Kamenev|Kamenev]] at the Second Party Congress of
