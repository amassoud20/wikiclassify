wealth is inherited along familial lines: the ability of poor Americans to overcome their situations is greatly or completely diminished {sfn|Marvin|2002|p=19}} Vonnegut also often laments social Darwinism, and a "survival of the fittest" view of society He points out that social Darwinism leads to a society that condemns its poor for their own misfortune, and fails to help them out of their
