event, indeed, would come about a few years later lt;ref name="Th127">Thucydides, [[s:History of the Peloponnesian War/Book 1#1:127|1 27]]</ref> At that time, the Athenians unhesitatingly followed Pericles' instructions In the first legendary oration Thucydides puts in his mouth, Pericles advised the Athenians not to yield to their opponents' demands, since they were militarily stronger lt;ref
